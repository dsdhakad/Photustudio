Who We Are

Artists Behind
the Lens

"We don't take photographs with cameras. We take them with our hearts, our minds, and our whole lives."

We are a passionate duo of visual storytellers based in India, specializing in weddings, portraits, fashion, and commercial photography. With over 8 years of experience, we've developed an eye that goes beyond the technical — we see light, emotion, and the stories hidden in split-second moments.

Every session is a deep collaboration. We don't direct — we discover. We don't pose — we play. The result? Images that feel alive, honest, and timeless.

Arjun & Priya Lead Photographers & Co-Founders
